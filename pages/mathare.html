MATHARI TEACHING AND REFERAL HOSPITAL

Kenya's Premier psychiatric Hospital.
Mathari National is specialized in Teaching, Referral, Training and Research.Founded in 1910, the hospital is the only government psychiatric hospital.
It is the only public institution that deals with mental health in Kenya.

Mathari National teaching and referral hospital is the largest mental health tertiary institution in East Africa.
The hospital runs an outpatient psychiatric department which runs 24hrs a day, 7days a week, 365days a year.
It has both in and out patient services. It not only deals with adults but also with children and teens
It deals with a wide range of mental health services from therapy to admission of severe cases.
